The Guide to Monetizing your content on StreackIt

StreackIt provides students an opportunity to make the most of our platform by being able to earn through content they enjoy creating. An incredible way of earning while you learn. Through this we wish to encourage students to not loose touch on what they love most apart from their academics.



Prerequisites

  • User must have a minimum of 50 followers. There is no lower restrictions on the number of posts, likes.
  • Users must be a registered student of the community (currently IIT Gandhinagar).
  • Monetizing however on the other hand will be mostly based on the number of likes, comments, posts. Monetization will also be subjected to restrictions on the subspace to wish you post. This means subspaces, which tend to be discussion based are treatly different. However this does not mean that such content won't be monetized. It simply means such content will ne treatly differently compared to other subspaces
